No More Miners
In
China
By Lausky

Trading in cryptocurrency has been thoroughly banned in China to maintain the Chinese yuan’s dominance. In February, China’s Supreme Court ruled that some virtual asset transactions can constitute illegal fundraising, paving the way for judicial prosecution of the crypto industry. The report attempts to examine and chronicle the crackdown and downfall of the cryptocurrency industry in China.
